Our Beginning

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiast Sarah Mitchell, Freshly Ground started as a small corner shop with a big dream: to bring authentic, artisanal coffee to Pretoria. What began as a single roaster and a passion for quality has grown into a beloved community hub.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great coffee starts with great relationships. That's why we work directly with small-scale farmers across Africa, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. Every bean is ethically sourced and carefully roasted in-house to bring out its unique character.

Our Community

More than just a coffee shop, we're a gathering place. Students study in our cozy corners, professionals hold meetings in our private booths, and friends reconnect over our signature blends. We host weekly coffee tastings, live music nights, and barista workshops.
